# Logo Integration Instructions

## Your Swaad-E-Sehat Logo

Please save your logo image as `logo.png` in the `public/` directory.

The logo should be:

- **Filename**: `logo.png`
- **Location**: `public/logo.png`
- **Format**: PNG with transparent background
- **Size**: Recommended 200x200 pixels or higher for crisp display

## Logo Description

Your logo features:

- **Circular Design**: Dark green circle with beige ornate label
- **Brand Name**: "SWAAD-E-SEHAT" in elegant serif font
- **Tagline**: "HAR BITE MEIN SEHAT KA ASLI SWAAD"
- **Product Image**: Traditional sweets/laddoos at the top
- **Disclaimer**: "100% AUTHENTIC NO PRESERVATIVES"
- **Colors**: Dark green, beige/cream, dark brown text

## Integration Points

The logo will be integrated into:

1. **Header**: Main navigation logo
2. **Footer**: Company branding
3. **Favicon**: Browser tab icon
4. **About Page**: Brand showcase

Once you upload the logo file, the website will automatically use it!
